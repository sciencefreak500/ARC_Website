Increasing the Value of Your Assets

Our Mission

Asset Remediation Corporation (ARC)

At ARC, our mission is to provide strategies, including market-based solutions, to monetize “toxic assets” (assets that are highly risky, difficult to value and nearly impossible to sell) and reduce the tax burden of high performing assets.

Our process enables our clients to enhance the value of their assets, improve their performance and increase the value of their firms.

How it works

ARC's primary product is three-phased (consisting of assessment, valuation and disposition strategies) and includes market-based solutions to monetize toxic assets currently residing on the balance sheets of financial institutions, insurance companies, hedge funds and other corporations.
We also offer services that provide for the orderly valuation and configuration of asset-backed investment portfolios internal control assessment, legal compliance and risk management, and financial advisory and management consulting.
