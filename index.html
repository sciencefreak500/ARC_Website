Our Mission

Asset Remediation Corporation (ARC)

At ARC, our mission is to provide market-based solutions to monetize “trouble assets” (high risk,  nearly impossible to sell assets) and reduce the tax burden of high performing assets. Our process enables our clients to enhance the value of their assets, and increase the performance and value of their firms.

ARC's primary product is three-phased (Assessment, Valuation and Disposition) in order to monetize toxic assets currently residing on the balance sheets of financial institutions, insurance companies, hedge funds and other corporations.

Phase 1 : Assessment

We assess the regulatory landscape for our clients and assess their portfolio

Phase 2: Valuation

We perform full asset valuations and manage them to minimize loss.

Phase 3: Disposition

We package the assets, and perform marketing, sales, and delivery.
